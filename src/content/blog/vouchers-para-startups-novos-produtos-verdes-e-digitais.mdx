**PROMOTING ENTITY**

Top BikeTours Portugal, Unipessoal, Lda

**PROJECT DESCRIPTION**

Vouchers for Startups - New Green and Digital Products

Application SM@RT_BOOKING 3301 - AAC No.10/C16-i02/2022

The project, promoted by Top Bike Tours Portugal Unipessoal, Lda, aims to
transform current customer interaction and relationship procedures throughout
the entire customer journey by using new digital technologies, enabling more
capable, integrated, and centralized management. Additionally, based on digital
marketing tools, it will promote the company and its products in various
markets, attracting and retaining new audiences. Besides contributing to digital
transformation, particularly in terms of internal organization, new processes,
and commercial strategy, positive scalability effects are also anticipated for a
business model characterized by a strong green and health/well-being component.

**OBJECTIVES**

- Promotion of business models, products, or digital services that reduce
  pollution impacts.
- Promotion of business models, products, or services with a clear digital
  component that are environmentally friendly and do not cause significant harm
  to any environmental objective as defined in Article 17 of Regulation (EU)
  2020/852 of the European Parliament and the Council (EU Taxonomy Regulation).
