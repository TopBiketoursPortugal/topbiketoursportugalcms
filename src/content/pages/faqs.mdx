          Suitable for inexperienced hikers with some fitness preparation.

          60% of the route. Suitable for hikers with fitness preparation who
          want to challenge themselves.

          distances between 15 km and 30 km and strong climbs up to 60% of the
          route. Suitable for experienced hikers.
