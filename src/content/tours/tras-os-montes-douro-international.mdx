# Trás-os-Montes & Douro international

## For those who enjoy the marvels of nature and would like to venture further

Trás-os-Montes and Alto Douro is a huge Portuguese province, belonging to Vila
Real and Bragança.

This area is considered by many as an authentic greenside paradise in the deep
valleys of the region, where you will find beautiful vineyards at the heart of
the Port and Douro wines.

The territory boosts breathtaking landscapes of natural beauty, revealing not
only a land rich in vineyards, but also in cereals, vegetables and fruits,
combining in a typical gastronomy, awarded the title of World Heritage.

**Route description**:

- Mostly steep. Up and down route.
- 100 % paved roads with very low traffic.
- 6392 meters of cumulative gap climbs

**Itinerary:**

Day 1: Porto – Pinhão (110 km, train transfer)

Day 2: Pinhão – Mirandela (79 km)

Day 3: Mirandela – Bragança (71 km)

Day 4: Bragança – Miranda do Douro (87 km)

Day 5: Miranda do Douro – Alfândega da Fé (98 km)

Day 6: Alfândega da Fé – Tua (74 km)

Day 7: Train transfer to Porto
